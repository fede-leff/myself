Everyone is telling the truth. The system is lying.

Margin does not bleed inside a department. It bleeds in the handoff. Procurement optimizes procurement. Operations optimizes operations. Sales optimizes sales. Each one is doing its job, and the transaction between them is nobody's job. That gap is where the dead lines, the missed dates, and the eroding margin live. I find the gap and I own it until it holds. Then I hand you a system that runs without me and I leave.
